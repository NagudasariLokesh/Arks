Careers at ARKS Micro Electronics Pvt. Ltd

Join our engineering team working on embedded systems, telemetry platforms, communication modules, and mission-critical electronics solutions for industrial and defence applications. We are looking for passionate innovators ready to build the next generation of advanced electronics technology.

Firmware Developer

Strong background in firmware architecture, protocol integration, and hardware-level troubleshooting.

Electronics Design Engineer
