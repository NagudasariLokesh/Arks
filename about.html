Company Overview

ARKS Micro Electronics India Pvt. Ltd. specializes in the design, development, and deployment of high-reliability embedded electronic systems for defense, aerospace support environments, telemetry, and industrial automation sectors.

With decades of engineering experience and strong collaboration with national research organizations, we deliver mission-ready solutions built for performance, precision, and long-term reliability.
